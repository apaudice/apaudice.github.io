About me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Milan, Italy. I work among the LAILA team led by professor Nicolò Cesa-Bianchi.
My recent research interests revolve around learning theory with focus on statistical learning, active learning and stochastic optimization.

I hold a PhD in Machine Learning, obtained at University of Milan under the supervision of professors Nicolò Cesa-Bianchi and Massimiliano Pontil.
My dissertation focuses on Active Learning for exact cluster recovery (manuscript, defense slides).

Research Interests

My research interests revolve around Learning Theory, and include more precisely:

  • Active Learning

  • Stochastic Optimization

  • Statistical Learning

  • Robust Learning
